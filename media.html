In The News

Glimpses of our ongoing programs, campaigns, and community impacts as comprehensively covered by the national and local press.

Healthcare & Diagnostics

Free Health Checkup Camp at Nangal

Providing free ECG, diabetes, and eye checkups to residents of Khajan Basti at the Shri Sanatan Mandir. Inaugurated by local MLA Shri Shyam Sharma.

Child Welfare

Cultural Program & Winter Relief

Organized a massive cultural event for children in Hari Nagar, complementing our winter relief efforts providing warm meals and clothes to the Mayapuri railway basti.

Winter Relief

Urgent Blanket Distribution

Battling severe cold waves across North India, Foundation Chairperson Sheetal Sharma led swift blanket distributions in Prayog Vihar alongside our free ongoing classes.

Industrial Health

Extensive Medical Relief for Workers

Our diagnostic camps saw huge attendance from factory and junk market workers seeking critical EEG, ECG, and blood pressure screenings directly within their communities.

Women's Health

Breaking Taboos: Menstrual Hygiene Drive

A major initiative distributing free sanitary pads across the Hari Nagar basti area while fiercely campaigning to eradicate societal myths surrounding menstrual hygiene.
