Our Journey

About Us

The Foundation of Change

Founded on the principle of unconditional service, the Jan Manas Foundation is a dedicated non-governmental organization built by a collective of passionate volunteers. Our driving force is the desire to rapidly bridge the gap between privilege and poverty in India's most vulnerable demographic segments.

Official Registrations & Transparency

Integrity and transparency sit at the core of our operations. The Jan Manas Foundation is officially registered under the NITI Aayog NGO Darpan portal, affirming our commitment to accountability. Furthermore, we are formally approved for tax exemptions under Sections 12AB and 80G of the Income Tax Act, allowing our benefactors to contribute confidently while receiving tangible fiscal benefits.

Our Vision for the Future

Our comprehensive mission spans far beyond basic charity. We aim to empower communities through education, deliver critical healthcare drives, protect environmental ecosystems, and fiercely champion women's empowerment and human rights. We believe that sustained, metric-driven acts of kindness are the only way to rewrite the future of underserved communities permanently.
