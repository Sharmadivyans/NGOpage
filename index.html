Who We Are

Registered for Transparency & Impact

The Jan Manas Foundation is formally registered under the NITI Aayog NGO Darpan portal. We prioritize institutional transparency and trackable metrics, ensuring that our supporters see their contributions translate into clear results. We are approved for tax exemptions under sections 12AB and 80G of the Income Tax Act.

Operating from our headquarters in Hari Nagar, New Delhi, our structural approach targets root-cause intervention through direct involvement in schools and deep community collaborations.

What We Do

Key Focus Areas

Education

Providing free coaching, stationery, school bags, and uniforms to children in slum areas, ensuring they have the tools they need to succeed and break the cycle of poverty.

Healthcare

Organizing free medical checkup camps, including ECG, diabetes, and eye checkups, to bring essential diagnostic services directly to underserved communities.

Women Empowerment

Conducting menstrual hygiene drives, distributing free sanitary pads, and campaigning fiercely to eradicate societal myths surrounding women's health.
